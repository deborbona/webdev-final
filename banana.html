Quarantine Baking

Banana Bread

Ingredients:

  • ⅓ cup melted coconut oil
  • ½ cup honey
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 or 4 overripe bananas
  • ¼ nut-milk
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1¾ cups white whole wheat flour
  • Optional: ½ cup nuts, dried fruit, or chocolate chips

    • Directions:

  • Pre-heat oven to 325 degrees Fahrenheit. Grease a pan and line the bottom with parchment paper.
  • In a large bowl, whisk the coconut oil and honey well.
  • In another bowl, mash your bananas with a fork. Scrape into the large bowl, add eggs and milk, and mix well.
  • Add the baking soda, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt. Mix well.
  • Stir in the flour in increments of ¼ cup, mixing in between those increments.
  • Fold in the nuts/fruit/chocolate.
  • Pour batter into your pan. Sprinkle some leftover cinnamon on top.
  • Bake in the oven for 1 hour. Cool completely before removing from the pan.

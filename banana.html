Quarantine Baking

Banana Bread

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup butter
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ⅔ cup chocolate chips

    • Directions:

  • Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  • Melt the butter in your microwave for 30 seconds.
  • Scrape the butter into a large bowl and whisk in the sugars.
  • Add the egg, vanilla, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Mix well.
  • Stir in the flour, but not too much— this will ensure a good structure.
  • Fold in the chocolate chips.
  • Bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes. Cool before removing from the pan.
  • Optional: sprinkle flaky sea salt on top for extra flavour.

Zucchini Muffins

Ingredients:

  • ⅓ cup melted coconut oil
  • ½ cup honey
  • 2 eggs
  • ⅔ cup buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 small or medium zucchini, grated
  • 1 ¾ cups white whole wheat flour
  • Optional: toasted nuts

    • Directions:

  • Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit. Grease a 12-cup muffin pan.
  • In a large bowl, combine the coconut oil and honey.
  • With a whisk, add the eggs and beat them in.
  • Mix in the buttermilk, cinnamon, baking powder, baking soda, salt, nutmeg, and vanilla.
  • Stir in the zucchini, then add the flour, then the nuts.
  • Pour mixture into your muffin pan. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes.
  • Cool completely before serving.

    • While you wait for the muffins to bake, get a pen and paper and draw any object close to you that inspires you. What about the object inspires you and how does it make you feel?

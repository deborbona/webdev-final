Quarantine Baking

Chocolate Chip Blondies

Ingredients:

  • 1½ cups brown sugar
  • ½ cup melted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

    • Directions:

  • Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Grease a pan and line the bottom with parchment paper.
  • In a large bowl, combine the brown sugar, butter, eggs, and vanilla.
  • Add the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  • Stir in the chocolate chips.
  • Pour batter into your pan. Bake for 18-20 minutes.
  • After cooling completely, cut into 16 squares.

    • While you wait for the blondies to bake, get out a pen and paper and write about 10 things you feel grateful for.

Raisin Scones

Go back to all recipes

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup cold butter, cubed
  • ¾ cup heavy cream, plus 2 extra tablespoons
  • 1 egg, plus 1 extra egg yolk
  • ½ cup raisins

    • Directions:

  • Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit. Line 1 cookie sheet with parchment paper.
  • In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt with a whisk.
  • With your hands, add the cubed butter and mix together roughly, pressing the butter and powders together.
  • In a separate bowl, whisk together the heavy cream and egg.
  • Add the wet mixture into the dry mixture, mixing roughly. Then add the raisins.
  • Put your dough onto a floured surface and knead well.
  • On your cookie sheet, form the dough into a wheel shape. Cut the wheel into 8 slices.
  • In another bowl, whisk the extra heavy cream and egg yolk.
  • Brush the heavy cream and yolk mixture on to the top of your cut wheel.
  • Bake in your oven for 25-30 minutes. Cool completely before serving.

    • While you wait for the scones to bake, get out a book and read a couple of pages. Let yourself be transported into another universe, but make sure you come back before your scones burn!

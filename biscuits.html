Anzac Biscuits

Ingredients:

  • ½ unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons boiling water
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ⅔ cup white sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • ¾ cup shredded coconut

    • Directions:

  • Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Line 3 cookie sheets with parchment paper.
  • In a microwave-safe bowl, put the butter and maple syrup in the microwave until the mixture bubbles.
  • With a whisk, add the boiling water and baking soda and mix well.
  • Mix in your sugar and salt.
  • Stir in the flour, oats, and coconut.
  • With an ice cream scoop, place 5 balls of cookie dough on two pans and 4 on the last pan.
  • Bake in the oven for 12 minutes. Cool completely before removing from the pans.

    • While you wait for the biscuits to bake, play some music and dance to your favourite songs. Don't be self-conscious and have fun!
